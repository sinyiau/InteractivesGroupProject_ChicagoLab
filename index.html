Sara Cooper

Methodology


○	TRR data was combined with CIT training data which included:
    ■	Officer name
    ■	Officer star number
    ■	Date of CIT certification
○	Each TRR report was coded (1,0) to indicate whether the reporting officer had CIT training before the event date
○	A separate table was created to deduplicate UoF events (multiple TRR reports are often made for the same event)
○	Each event was coded (1,0) for whether or not a CIT trained officer was involved in the response
○	Each event was additionally coded (1,0) for its relation to a mental health issue
○	Additional columns were added to capture the event date as well as several other criteria for exploratory data analysis (Beat, TRR Type, Subject Injury)
○	For the pie charts the total percentage of CIT trained officer responses to mental health and overall UoF incidents were calculated accordingly:
    ■	COUNTIFS([CIT Trained Officer?],1,[Mental Health],1)/COUNTIF([Mental Health],1)
    ■	COUNTIF(CIT Trained Officer?,1]/COUNT([Year])
○	For the volume over time chart, the proportion of UoF incidents with CIT trained officers present for each year were calculated accordingly:
    ■	COUNTIFS([Year],year,[CIT Trained Officer?],1,[Mental Health?],1)/COUNTIFS([Year],year,[Mental Health?],1)
    ■	COUNTIFS([Year],year,[CIT Trained Officer?],1)/COUNTIF([Year],year)
